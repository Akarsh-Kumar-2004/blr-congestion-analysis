📊 SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE (1 PICTURE)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   Event Details (cause, priority, zone, hour, etc.)
            ↓
   LightGBM Quantile Regression (p50 + p90 models)
            ↓
   Predicted Clearance Time (minutes)
            ↓
   Impact Formula: priority + road_closure + duration_bucket
            ↓
   Resource Recommendation: officers/barricades/tow/diversion
            ↓
   Officer sees: "Deploy 8 officers + 6 barricades + diversion YES"
            ↓
   [Officer takes action]
            ↓
   [Incident resolves, actual time recorded]
            ↓
   Feedback Loop: pred (38 min) vs actual (42 min) → retraining quarterly
